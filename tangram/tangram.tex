\begin{enumerate}
    \item[Step 1.] Take a sheet of paper and cut out a square.
    \item[Step 2.] Cut the square into two congruent triangles.
    \item[Step 3.] Take one of the triangles and cut it again into two congruent triangles.
    \item[Step 4.] Take the larger triangle that is left. Find the midpoint of its longest side and press the paper there to mark it.
    \item[Step 5.] Fold the vertex opposite to the longest side of the triangle down to the middle point you marked. Cut off the small triangle made by the fold.
    \item[Step 6.] Fold the trapezium to get two congruent trapeziums.
    \item[Step 7.] From one trapezium, cut out one square and one right-angled triangle.
    \item[Step 8.] From the other trapezium, cut out one parallelogram and one right-angled triangle.
\end{enumerate}
